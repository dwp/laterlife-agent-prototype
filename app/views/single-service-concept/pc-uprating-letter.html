Pension Credit uprating letter

Your Pension Credit amount

Dear Savita Kaur,

Your Pension Credit is going up on 10 April 2026. This is because of the annual increase to State Pension and Pension Credit.

Your new regular payment amount will be £23.90 every 4 weeks.

Your first payment after 10 April 2026 may be lower as it includes some weeks at the old rate and some at the new rate.

How we worked out your new Pension Credit amount

Based on your circumstances, you will get Pension Credit to top up your income to £238.00 a week.

On 10 April 2026 your State Pension is going up to £171.75 a week. This means you will get £11.95 Pension Credit a week.
